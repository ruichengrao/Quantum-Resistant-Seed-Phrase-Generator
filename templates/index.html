About Our Technology

Leveraging cutting-edge cryptographic standards, our seed generator produces a unique and secure mnemonic phrase and wallet seed that form the cornerstone of crypto security.

Our technology is built upon industry standards such as BIP39 and utilizes advanced algorithms to ensure that your digital assets are safeguarded by state-of-the-art security measures.

How It Works

1. Entropy Generation: High-quality random data is produced as the foundation for your seed.

2. Data Conversion: The random data is transformed into binary format.

3. Checksum & Mapping: A checksum is computed and the data is segmented, mapping to words from the BIP39 list.

4. Wallet Seed Derivation: The mnemonic is processed using scrypt to derive a secure wallet seed.
